Signal Demos contain audio and video clips referred to in the text with a double CD symbol.

Programs contain all MATLAB programs referred to in Chapters 2-14 and Appendix A of the book.

Additional Examples contain additional examples to Chapters 2-14 of the book.

Review Materials give a brief review about the prerequisite knowledge of each chapter.

Applications give a brief description about several applications of digital signal processing.

Introduction to MATLAB gives a brief introduction to the commonly used functions of MATLAB.

FAQ's contain answers to some frequently asked questions which the reader may encounter when studying this book.
